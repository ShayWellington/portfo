About Me

Kia ora! I am an impassioned, young creative located in Wellington, New Zealand. At 21 years old, my almost 4 years of Design and Web Development education, as well as the many extracurricular endeavours I undertook throughout my studies, has given me the tools to be a capable designer and developer.

Since a young age, I have had a deep admiration and adoration for the arts and in particular, digital illustration and painting. This has provided me with a developed understanding of visual art and the principals and elements that are utilised (or in some cases, thrown right out the window) to create compelling and successful pieces of graphic art.

My desire in life is one with no end point. I dream to continually hone the craft that I have spent my entire life pursuing. To never stop creating, making and moving forward. To create is to breathe and I crave for every strike of inspiration to lead me to a new, exciting visual outcome.
